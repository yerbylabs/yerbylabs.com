IronBeak:
The Avian Chronicles

A gamified bird-watching journal for Android. Log sightings, earn XP, complete daily quests, and build your life list — all without an account or internet connection.

🦅

Expedition Log

Record every sighting with species, count, behavior, habitat, and field notes. Your data stays on your device.

⚔️

RPG Progression

Earn XP and level up your birder rank. Complete daily and weekly quests to keep the streak alive.

🏆

Achievements

Unlock badges for milestones like your 50th species, 100 sightings, or spotting a rare bird.

📍

Haunts

Tag your favourite birding spots and track which species you've seen at each location over time.
