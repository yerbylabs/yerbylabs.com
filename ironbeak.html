What awaits you

Crafted for the Field Naturalist

📖

Field Journal

Your personal life list, built in the field. Every confirmed sighting is recorded with species, date, habitat, and notes — a permanent record that grows with you. Illustrated cards bring each entry to life.

🧭

Daily Expeditions

Embark on expeditions to identify birds in your region. Complete daily quests, earn XP, and climb the ornithologist ranks.

🦉

Ask Witters

Not sure what you're looking at? Describe the bird — size, color, behavior, habitat — and Witters, IronBeak's resident field ornithologist, will narrow it down for you. Powered by AI, grounded in real field craft.

🎯

Mastery Levels

Track how well you know each species. Log more sightings to climb from First Sighting to Habitat Specialist — earning bonus XP each time you level up. Reach Level 5 and unlock a Mastery Seal badge.

🏆

Badges & Ranks

Rise from Nestling to Master Ornithologist as you earn XP in the field. Collect badges across six categories — from Quest Mastery and Species Collection to Rarity Hunter and beyond.

🐦

636 Species

A curated catalog of North American birds across regional packs — from common backyard visitors to legendary rarities.
