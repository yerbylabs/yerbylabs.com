Privacy

Delete My Data

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data from IronBeak: The Avian Chronicles. We will process your request within 30 days.

What will be deleted

  • Your account and profile (display name, email address)
  • All bird sighting records and journal entries stored on our servers
  • Your game progress, XP, achievements, and streak data stored on our servers
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

What will not be deleted

  • Data stored locally on your device (you can delete this by uninstalling the app)
  • Anonymized, aggregated data that cannot be linked back to you
  • Records required for legal compliance or fraud prevention

How to request deletion

Send an email to admin@yerbylabs.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your IronBeak account. We will confirm receipt and complete the deletion within 30 days.
