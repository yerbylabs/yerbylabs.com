IronBeak: The Avian Chronicles

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026  ·  Developer: Yerby Labs LLC  ·  yerbylabs@gmail.com

Short version: Almost everything you do in IronBeak stays on your device. We don't collect personal data, don't run ads, and don't sell your information.

Overview

IronBeak: The Avian Chronicles is a bird journaling app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Data We Collect

Data stored locally on your device

All core app data — your sightings, journal entries, species list, Haunts, badges, XP, streaks, and settings — is stored only on your device using local storage. This data is never transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly use a feature that requires it (such as Flock social features).

Data collected when using Flock (optional social features)

If you choose to use the Flock feature to connect with other birders, the following data is stored on our servers:

  • A display name you choose
  • Your life list species count and recent sighting activity (not the full content of your journal)
  • Your device's push notification token (if you grant notification permission)

Location data

If you choose to use the "Use My Location" feature when logging a sighting, the app requests your device location to determine your county and state. Your precise GPS coordinates are never stored. Only the county and state name are saved with the sighting. Location data is never transmitted to our servers.

Crash and error reporting

The app uses Sentry to collect anonymous crash reports. These reports include device type, OS version, and a stack trace of the error. They do not include your personal data or sighting content.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any identifying information unless you create a Flock account.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.
  • We do not track your location in the background.
  • We do not access your contacts, camera roll, or microphone unless you explicitly grant permission for a specific in-app action.

In-App Purchases

IronBeak offers optional in-app purchases (Haunts unlock, species data packs). Purchases are processed by Google Play. We do not store your payment information.

Children's Privacy

IronBeak is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at yerbylabs@gmail.com and we will delete it.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your local data remains on your device until you uninstall the app or use the "Reset Everything" option in Settings. If you have a Flock account, you may request deletion of your server-side data by contacting us at yerbylabs@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via an in-app notice. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
