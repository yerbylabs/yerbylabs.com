Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 13, 2026

YerbyLabs ("we," "us," or "our") operates the IronBeak: The Avian Chronicles mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the App's core functionality:

  • Bird sighting data — species name, date, logging method (manual, photo, or audio), and any optional notes you enter.
  • Region — your selected home region (e.g., "Midwest" or "Pacific NW"). We do not collect precise GPS coordinates. If you grant location permission in a future update, we will derive a region-level string and immediately discard the raw coordinates.
  • Audio recordings — if you use the audio logging feature, recordings are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.
  • Photos — if you attach a photo to a sighting, it is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • App usage data — XP, streak counts, badge progress, and mastery levels, stored locally on your device.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account credentials unless you explicitly create an account in a future version of the App.
  • We do not collect precise GPS location data.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or third-party analytics trackers.

3. How We Use Your Information

All data collected is used solely to:

  • Display your field journal, sighting history, and progress within the App.
  • Calculate XP, streaks, and badge achievements.
  • Provide region-appropriate bird species for expeditions.

4. Data Storage

All sighting data, photos, and audio recordings are stored locally on your device using the device's secure storage. We do not operate a cloud database for personal sighting data at this time. If cloud sync is introduced in a future version, this policy will be updated and you will be notified before any data is transmitted.

5. Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions:

  • Microphone — used only when you choose to log a sighting via audio. Recordings are stored locally and not transmitted.
  • Camera — used only when you choose to log a sighting via photo. Photos are stored locally and not transmitted.
  • Notifications — used to deliver optional daily quest reminders. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

You may deny any permission; doing so will disable the associated feature but will not affect other App functionality.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the address below and we will promptly delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
